Sol Souls was a generative NFT collection on the Solana blockchain with a community worth building for. The Afterlife hub is the social layer that ties holders together — a Next.js portal that connects to the Solana network via a DataHub RPC endpoint and surfaces collection data, ownership, and community context in one place.

The project reflects the design energy of the Solana NFT era: move fast, ship something useful for your community, and iterate. The stack is TypeScript throughout, with a monorepo structure separating the Next.js frontend from shared utilities. For anyone interested in how NFT-gated community sites were architected in that period — before the tooling matured into what it is today — the codebase is a useful artifact.
